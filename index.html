About me

I obtained my Ph.D. degree in statistics at Department of Technology, Operations, and Statistics, Stern School of Business, New York University, advised by Professor Xi Chen.

Before joining NYU, I received my Bachelor's degree in Economics and Finance at School of Economics and Management, Tsinghua University and Bachelor's degree in Mathematics at Department of Mathematics, Tsinghua University in 2018.

I will join Citadel LLC as a quantitative researcher from January 2023.
