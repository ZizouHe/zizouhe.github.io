About me

I am currently working at Citadel, Global Quantitative Strategies as a quantitative researcher.

Before joining Citadel, I obtained my Ph.D. degree in statistics at Department of Technology, Operations, and Statistics, Stern School of Business, New York University, advised by Professor Xi Chen in 2022.

I received my Bachelor's degree in Economics and Finance at School of Economics and Management, Tsinghua University and Bachelor's degree in Mathematics at Department of Mathematics, Tsinghua University in 2018.
